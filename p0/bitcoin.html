Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a decentralized form of digital money, independent of nation-states and corporations. Unlike traditional currencies, it operates on a peer-to-peer network, without reliance on financial intermediaries such as banks. Bitcoin transactions are recorded on a public ledger called the blockchain, ensuring transparency and security. Its limited supply and decentralized nature make it a unique asset, often viewed as a store of value and a hedge against inflation, attracting both investors and enthusiasts globally.
