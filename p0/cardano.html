    Cardano

    Cardano is a third-generation blockchain platform that focuses on scalability, sustainability, and flexibility. As an open-source and decentralized platform, it uses a proof-of-stake (PoS) consensus mechanism called Ouroboros. The native cryptocurrency, ADA, powers the network, enabling transactions, staking, and governance. Cardano aims to address the limitations of previous blockchain systems by offering an environmentally friendly alternative with a more efficient and secure approach to decentralized applications and smart contracts. Its layered architecture allows for easy upgrades and adaptability to future technological advancements.
